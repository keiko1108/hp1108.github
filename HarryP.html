Characteristics

Personality: Harry is brave, determined, loyal, humble, and self-sacrificing. He is guided by his strong sense of right and wrong.

Appearance: Harry has jet-black hair, bright green eyes, and a lightning bolt scar on his forehead. He also wears round glasses.

House

Harry Potter was sorted into Gryffindor House at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Family

Harry is the only child of James and Lily Potter. He was orphaned as an infant and raised by his aunt and uncle, the Dursleys.

Skills

  • Gifted wizard with talent in Defense Against the Dark Arts
  • Seeker and Captain of the Gryffindor Quidditch team
  • Exceptional flyer on a broomstick
  • Skilled in casting the Patronus charm
