Characteristics

Personality: Hermione is intelligent, hardworking, and determined. She values education and is often the voice of reason among her friends. She is also courageous and fiercely loyal to her loved ones.

Appearance: Hermione has brown hair, brown eyes, and is known for her bushy hair and prominent front teeth (which she later has magically fixed).

House

Hermione was sorted into Gryffindor House at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where she became one of the brightest students.

Family

Hermione is the only child of Mr. and Mrs. Granger, who are both Muggles (non-magical). She grew up in a loving family, and her parents are dentists.

Skills

  • Exceptional intelligence and magical talent
  • Expert in spell casting, charms, and potions
  • Skilled in various branches of magic, especially time manipulation (Time-Turner)
  • Brave in the face of danger, especially when it comes to her friends
