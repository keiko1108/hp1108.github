Characteristics

Personality: Ron is loyal, brave, and humorous. He often acts as the voice of reason among his friends but can be insecure at times.

Appearance: Ron has red hair, freckles, and blue eyes. He is tall and lanky, often seen with a slightly awkward posture.

House

Ron was sorted into Gryffindor House at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he made many lifelong friends.

Family

Ron is the sixth child of Molly and Arthur Weasley. He has five older brothers—Bill, Charlie, Percy, Fred, and George—and a younger sister, Ginny.

Skills

  • Great wizard and a talented chess player
  • Seeker on the Gryffindor Quidditch team
  • Brave in the face of danger
  • Skilled in using magic, although often insecure about his abilities
